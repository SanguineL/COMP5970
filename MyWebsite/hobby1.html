Basketball

The fastest growing sport in the world.
I love to play basketball.
Rules
2 points per basket, unless behind the arc line. Then it is 3 points. Team with the most points after two 20-minute halves wins.

Fun Facts

Origin
Basketball was created in Springfield, Massachusetts, in 1891.
NBA
There are 32 teams in the NBA, which is the most popular professional basketball league in the world. The NBA uses 4 12-minute quarters instead of 2 halves.
